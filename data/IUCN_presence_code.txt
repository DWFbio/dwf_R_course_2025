Code	Definition
1	Extant (species is currently present and known to exist in the area)
2	Probably Extant (species is likely present, but there is some uncertainty)
3	Possibly Extant (species may be present, but data is insufficient to confirm)
4	Possibly Extinct (species was previously known in the area but may now be extinct)
5	Extinct (species is no longer found in the area)
6	Presence Uncertain (insufficient data to determine whether the species occurs in the area)
